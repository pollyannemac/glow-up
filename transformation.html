C-Section Scar Massage

Once your scar is fully healed (usually 6+ months post-op), gentle scar tissue massage helps break adhesions, reduce the "shelf" appearance, improve sensation, and restore tissue mobility.

How to do it (5 minutes daily):

  • Apply oil or moisturiser to the area
  • Using 2 fingers, massage in small circles along the length of the scar
  • Then massage perpendicular to the scar (up and down across it)
  • Gently lift and roll the scar tissue between fingers
  • Try side-to-side motions — moving the skin over the underlying tissue
  • Pressure should be firm but comfortable — stop if painful
  • Work along the full scar, including the corners

Tips: Best done after a warm shower when tissue is soft. Consistency matters more than pressure. Even 2-3 minutes is beneficial. If you feel tightness or pulling around your scar during core exercises, this is especially important for you.
